Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Motivo is designed to be a safe, supportive space. Here's how we handle your data:

  • What We Collect: Only the information you provide (like your mood or chat messages). No sensitive personal data is required.

  • Your Rights: You can request deletion of your data at any time. We respect your privacy choices.
  • Security: We use industry-standard security to protect your information.

If you have questions, contact us at support@motivo.com.
